## Migrant/discrimination background

13. Berry, J. W. (1997). *Immigration, acculturation, and adaptation*. Applied Psychology. https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1464-0597.1997.tb01087.x
14. Pascoe, E. A., & Smart Richman, L. (2009). *Perceived discrimination and health: A meta-analytic review*. Psychological Bulletin. https://doi.org/10.1037/a0016059

## Note

The Pascoe & Smart Richman (2009) source is useful for substantive background: perceived discrimination is linked to stress and health outcomes. It should not be used as direct evidence for MAPS item-level DIF; that claim must come from the current MAPS analysis or MAPS-specific prior work.
